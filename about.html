I’m a visual designer, artist, and current resident of Seattle.

Most recently, I was designing things for the Museum of Pop Culture in Seattle Center. Before I moved to Seattle, I obtained my BFA in Design at Northeastern University and then spent a few years working at a beer company, an ad agency, and an education-focused nonprofit.

I want to put my motion design skills, digital knowledge, and print experience to use and contribute to the world in a meaningful, inclusive way. In my down time, I’m also running an Etsy shop where you can buy some prints of my watercolors.
