Forget Me Not

Under direction of R. Kent Millard | UI/UX | Branding | Web Design | Fall 2016


Forget Me Not is an app that reminds users to water their plants. This responsive project began with sketching, was user-tested using Balsamiq mockups, and refined in InVision and Adobe Experience Design in both mobile and web prototypes. For a more in-depth look at the project, please email me.
